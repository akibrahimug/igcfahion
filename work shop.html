WORKSHOPS

PAPER FIG FOUNDATION 2017-NOW

Once a month we go to Kasese in the western parts of Uganda at the Paper Fig Foundation Sew school to do a masterclass in different techqnies of fashion. PFF is a theroporist arm of Laurie Dejong the founder and CEO of LDJ productions, a New York based productions Company. We met Laurie during Kampala Fashion Week and have been working together ever since to bring masterclasses to the students of Kasese.

MILANDILA MASTERCLASS 2018

We worked with milandila FOUNDATION. to teach the youth of wakiso, Bulabakulu village the different tecqinies of sewing and tailoring.

LOCAL KID ORGANIZATION

We partner with Local Kid to enable the parents of Kisoro, Batwa community to have income generating skills like tailoring, we have managed to teach them how to make duffel bags that they make and local kid helps to sell and in turn they can use the money to provide for their families.
